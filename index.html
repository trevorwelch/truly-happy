Artist Statement:

I wrote these songs at a time when many of the narratives I had believed about myself were thrown into question. I became transfixed by the idea of self and the inconsistency of self and reached for imagery to express how I could be many things all at once. In meditating on familiar landscapes and metaphors, I was able to explore my personal dissonance and begin to accept the multitude of selves that I am. - Levi

'Some Rivers Go Forever' by Truly Happy, out on Excellent Living Records

This album is the first release coming out on `xnlvg` aka eeeii aka excellent living. I've dreamed of doing a cassette label for some time now, and now's the time! Levi and I recorded this album over a week in Dripping Springs, TX. It felt like some of the purest music I've ever gotten to be a part of, and the decisions we made to sculpt the songs into what they became were all natural and reflexive. I love this album and it reflects a lot of the values that I hope to portray with Excellent Living recs into the future. ~Trevor
